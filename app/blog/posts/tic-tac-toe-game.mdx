Design a Tic-Tac-Toe game for two players that allows them to play on a 3×3 grid, 
take alternate turns, validate moves, and determine the winner or a draw based on the game state.

![tic-tac-toe](/tic-tac-toe/tic-tac-toe.png)

---

### Functional Requirements

1. Each player is assigned a unique symbol (e.g., X and O).

2. Players should be able to make a move by selecting a cell (row, column). The system must ensure the selected cell 
is not already occupied. Moves should be recorded and reflected on the board.

3. The system should enforce alternate turns between players. It should always indicate whose turn it is.

4. The system should check after every move whether a player has won. A player wins if they complete a full row, a full 
column, or a diagonal.

5. The system should detect a draw when all cells are filled and no player has won.
