Design a music streaming service like Spotify that can handle millions of users and 
billions of music streams every day ensuring low latency and high availability.

![spotify](/spotify/spotify.svg)

---

### Functional Requirements

1. **Search:** Users can search for songs, artists, albums, and playlists.

2. **Music Streaming:** Users can stream songs in real time.

3. **Playlists:** Users can create, share, and modify playlists.

---

### Non-Functional Requirements


1. **Scalability:** The system should handle 600 million monthly active users (MAU) globally and stream millions of 
songs concurrently.

2. **Low Latency:** Real-time streaming must have low latency for a seamless user experience.

3. **High Availability:** The system must be available at all times with minimal downtime.

4. **Global Reach:** Support users from different geographic regions, potentially leveraging CDNs to serve audio files faster.
