Build a comprehensive solution for efficiently managing a parking lot. The system should automate 
key processes such as vehicle entry, ticket generation, intelligent spot allocation, real-time 
occupancy tracking, and seamless vehicle exit, ensuring a smooth and reliable parking experience.

![parking-lot](/parking-lot.svg)

---

### Functional Requirements

1. Support parking for motorcycles, cars, and trucks.

2. Maintain three spot types (compact, regular, oversized) with appropriate capacity.

3. Assign spots based on vehicle size.

4. Calculate fees using vehicle size and parking duration, with time-of-day rate variations.
<br/>
---

### Sequence Flow

Let's discuss the step by step flow to identify the core entities involved:

1. `Vehicle (Motorcycle, Car, Truck)` arrives at the entry gate. The `ParkingLot` system captures basic details such 
as `licenseNumber`, `vehicleType` and `arrivalTime`.

2. The `ParkingLot` evaluates the `vehicleType` and searches across its managed `Map<VehicleType, List<ParkingSpot>>` to find the best available match `ParkingSpot` (`CompactSpot`, `RegularSpot`, or `OversizedSpot`).
